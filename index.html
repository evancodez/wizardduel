How to Play

Hold left-click and scrawl a glyph in the air, release to cast. Sloppy is fine — the wand forgives. You move slower and can't turn while drawing, so pick your moment.

You can see your foe's glyph forming in real time — and they see yours. Read it. Counter it. Or right-click mid-draw to cancel and feint.
Shield eats bolts & flame but shatters under stone. Mirror hurls projectiles back but lightning pierces it. Lightning interrupts a mid-draw rival. Healing is strong — and very punishable. Stones crack shields. The Grip steals rocks, debris… and wizards.
